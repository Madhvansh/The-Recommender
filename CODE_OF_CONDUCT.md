# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and
expression, experience, nationality, race, caste, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Discuss evidence and technical work rather than attacking people.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's private information.
- Accept responsibility and learn from mistakes.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, trolling,
and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method on the maintainer's
GitHub profile. The maintainer may edit or remove content and restrict
participation when necessary. Reports will be handled as confidentially as
practical, and retaliation is prohibited.

Adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
